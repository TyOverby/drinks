<th style="background-color: moccasin"><a href="/wiki/IBA_Official_Cocktail" class="mw-redirect" title="IBA Official Cocktail">IBA</a> specified ingredients*</th>
<td class="ingredient" style="background-color: moccasin">
<ul>
<li>5 cl <a href="/wiki/Vodka" title="Vodka">Vodka</a></li>
<li>1 cl <a href="/wiki/Kahlua" class="mw-redirect" title="Kahlua">Kahlua</a></li>
<li>Sugar syrup (according to individual preference of sweetness)</li>
<li>1 short strong Espresso</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<th style="background-color: moccasin">Preparation</th>
<td style="background-color: moccasin">Pour ingredients into shaker filled with ice, shake vigorously, and strain into chilled martini glass</td>

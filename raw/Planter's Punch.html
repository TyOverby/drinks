Planter's Punch IBA Official Cocktail Planter's Punch Type Mixed drink Primary alcohol by volume
  • Rum
Served On the rocks; poured over ice Standard garnish

Cherry and Pineapple slice

Standard drinkware
Highball glass IBA specified ingredients*
  • 4.5cl Dark rum
  • 3.5 cl Fresh orange juice
  • 3.5 cl Fresh pineapple juice
  • 2 cl Fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cl Grenadine syrup
  • 1 cl Sugar syrup
  • 3 or 4 dashes Angostura bitters
Preparation Pour all ingredients, except the bitters, into shaker filled with ice. Shake well. Pour into large glass, filled with ice. Add Angostura bitters, "on top". Garnish with cocktail cherry and pineapple.

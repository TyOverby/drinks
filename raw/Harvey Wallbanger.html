Harvey Wallbanger IBA Official Cocktail Type Mixed drink Primary alcohol by volume
  • Vodka
Served On the rocks; poured over ice Standard garnish

orange slice and maraschino cherry

Standard drinkware
Highball glass IBA specified ingredients*
  • 4.5 cL (3 parts) Vodka
  • 1.5 cL (1 part) Galliano
  • 9 cL (6 parts) fresh orange juice
Preparation Stir the vodka and orange juice with ice in the glass, then float the Galliano on top. Garnish and serve.

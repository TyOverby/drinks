Margarita IBA Official Cocktail Type Cocktail Primary alcohol by volume
  • Tequila
Served On the rocks; poured over ice Standard garnish

Salt on the rim

Standard drinkware
Margarita glass IBA specified ingredients*
  • 3.5 cL (7 parts) tequila
  • 2 cL (4 parts) Cointreau
  • 1.5 cL (3 parts) lime juice
Preparation Rub the rim of the glass with the lime slice to make the salt stick to it. Take care to moisten only the outer rim and sprinkle the salt on it. The salt should present to the lips of the imbiber and never mix into the cocktail. Shake the other ingredients with ice, then carefully pour into the glass. * Margarita recipe at International Bartenders Association

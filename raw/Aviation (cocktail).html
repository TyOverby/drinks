Standard drinkware
Cocktail glass IBA specified ingredients*
  • 4.5 cl gin
  • 1.5 cl lemon juice
  • 1.5 cl maraschino liqueur
Preparation Add all ingredients into cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well and strain into cocktail glass. Garnish with a cherry. Some recipes include crème de violette or Creme Yvette.

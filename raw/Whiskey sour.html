Cocktail glass IBA specified ingredients*
  • 4.5 cl (3 parts) Bourbon whiskey
  • 3 cl (2 parts) fresh lemon juice
  • 1.5 cl (1 part) Gomme syrup
  • dash egg white (optional)
Preparation Shake with ice. Strain into chilled glass, garnish and serve. Notes If served 'On the rocks', strain ingredients into old-fashioned glass filled with ice. Whiskey Sour recipe at DrinkBoy IBA

<th style="background-color: moccasin"><a href="/wiki/IBA_Official_Cocktail" class="mw-redirect" title="IBA Official Cocktail">IBA</a> specified ingredients*</th>
<td class="ingredient" style="background-color: moccasin">
<ul>
<li>4 cl gin</li>
<li>1.5 cl lemon juice</li>
<li>1 cl simple syrup</li>
<li>1.5 cl Creme de Mure(blackberry liqueur)</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<th style="background-color: moccasin">Preparation</th>
<td style="background-color: moccasin">Fill glass with crushed ice. Build gin, lemon juice and simple syrup over. Stir, and then pour blackberry liqueur over in a circular fashion to create marbling effect. Garnish with two blackberries and lemon slice.</td>
</tr>

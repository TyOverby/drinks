<th style="background-color: moccasin"><a href="/wiki/IBA_Official_Cocktail" class="mw-redirect" title="IBA Official Cocktail">IBA</a> specified ingredients*</th>
<td class="ingredient" style="background-color: moccasin">
<ul>
<li>4 cL (1 part) <a href="/wiki/Brandy" title="Brandy">Brandy</a></li>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<th style="background-color: moccasin">Preparation</th>
<td style="background-color: moccasin">Pour brandy and ginger ale directly into highball glass with ice cubes. Stir gently. Garnish with lemon zest. If desired, add dashes of Angostura Bitter.</td>

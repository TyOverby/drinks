  • Whiskey
Served On the rocks; poured over ice Standard garnish
  • Orange twist
  • cocktail cherry
Standard drinkware
Old Fashioned glass IBA specified ingredients*
  • 4.5 cL Bourbon or Rye whiskey
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 sugar cube
  • Few dashes plain water
Preparation Place sugar cube in old fashioned glass and saturate with bitters, add a dash of plain water.

Muddle until dissolved.
Fill the glass with ice cubes and add whiskey.
Garnish with orange twist, and a cocktail cherry.

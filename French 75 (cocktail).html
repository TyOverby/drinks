French 75 IBA Official Cocktail Type Wine cocktail Primary alcohol by volume
  • Champagne
Standard drinkware
Champagne flute IBA specified ingredients*
  • 3cl Cognac or gin
  • 2 dashes simple syrup
  • 1.5cl lemon juice
  • 6cl Champagne
Preparation Combine Cognac, syrup, and lemon juice in a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake vigorously and strain into a chilled champagne glass. Top up with Champagne. Stir gently.

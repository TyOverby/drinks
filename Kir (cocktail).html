Wine glass (white) IBA specified ingredients*
  • 9 cl (9 parts) white wine
  • 1 cl (1 part) crème de cassis
Preparation Add the crème de cassis to the bottom of the glass, then top up with wine. Notes A recipe can be found at the International Bartenders Association website. * Kir recipe at International Bartenders Association

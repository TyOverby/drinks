<th style="background-color: moccasin"><a href="/wiki/IBA_Official_Cocktail" class="mw-redirect" title="IBA Official Cocktail">IBA</a> specified ingredients*</th>
<td class="ingredient" style="background-color: moccasin">
<ul>
<li>5 cl <a href="/wiki/Cacha%C3%A7a" title="Cachaça">cachaça</a></li>
<li>Half a <a href="/wiki/Lime_(fruit)" title="Lime (fruit)">lime</a> cut into 4 wedges</li>
<li>2 teaspoons <a href="/wiki/Sugar" title="Sugar">sugar</a></li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<th style="background-color: moccasin">Preparation</th>
<td style="background-color: moccasin">Place lime and sugar into old fashioned glass and muddle (mash the two ingredients together using a <a href="/wiki/Muddler" title="Muddler">muddler</a> or a wooden spoon). Fill the glass with crushed ice and add the Cachaça.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<th>Notes</th>
<td>A wide variety of fresh fruits can be used in place of lime. In the absence of cachaça, vodka can be used, making a <a href="/wiki/Caipiroska" title="Caipiroska">caipiroska</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Brazilian_Drinks:_Caipirnha_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Brazilian_Drinks:_Caipirnha-1">[1]</a></sup></td>
</tr>
